Any Style — Any Length — Any Time

Technology built specifically for barbers and barbershops. Get discovered by more clients. Book more appointments. Make more money. Get started today.

SCHEDULING

Clients can book with you directly based on your availability. You can also create appointments for your clients who haven’t downloaded the app. You can view all of your appointments in one place as they are scheduled.

APOINTMENTS

Receive notifications when a client books or cancels an appointment. Earn back lost revenue by charging clients for last minute cancellations and no-shows. Clients will be notified of any potential cancellation and no-show fees when they book.

CLIENT MANAGEMENT

Keep notes about individual clients and view their history of past visits. Send important announcements to all of your clients at once. Let them know of any deals and promotions, or if you have to leave the shop for vacation or any other reason.

BRANDED PROFILE

Create your own custom profile link that you can share with your clients and market yourself. Clicking this link will prompt the client to download the app. Once they have signed up, they will immediately be taken to your profile.
